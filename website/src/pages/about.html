🏢 About Us

Making Hotel Management Simple

We believe every hotel in India — from a 10-seat dhaba to a 200-room chain — deserves affordable, powerful management software.

🎯 Our Mission

Empower Every Indian Hotelier

Most hotel management software is expensive, complex, and designed for large chains. Small and mid-size hotels — the backbone of India's hospitality industry — are left with pen-and-paper or basic apps that don't scale.

Tulasi Hotels was built to change that. We provide an all-in-one platform that covers billing, kitchen operations, staff management, inventory, reservations, and analytics — at a price that even a small restaurant can afford.

🇮🇳
Built for India

GST-compliant. Hindi & regional language support. UPI payments. Works on ₹8,000 phones. 100% offline capability for areas with poor connectivity.

The Problem We Solve

Hotel owners juggle 5-10 different tools — or none at all.

📝

Paper Registers

Bills written by hand. No records at month-end. Tax filing is a nightmare. Pen-and-paper simply can't scale.

🧩

Scattered Tools

One app for billing, another for inventory, Excel for staff records, WhatsApp for coordination. Nothing talks to each other.

💸

Expensive Software

Enterprise PMS solutions charge ₹5,000-15,000/month. Small hotels can't justify the cost — and the software is complex to learn.

What We Stand For

Simplicity

Offline-First

India's internet is unreliable. Our app works fully offline — billing, orders, inventory, everything. Data syncs when you're back online.

⚙️ Technology

Built With Modern Technology

We use cutting-edge tools to deliver a fast, reliable, and secure experience across all platforms.

  • Flutter — single codebase for Android, Web, and Windows
  • Firebase — real-time database, authentication, cloud functions
  • Firestore — NoSQL database with offline sync
  • Cloud Functions — serverless backend for notifications and scheduled tasks
  • Progressive Web App — installable from browser, works offline
  • Material Design 3 — modern, accessible UI components
