The Problems We Solve

Real challenges faced by hotel & restaurant owners every day.

Paper registers & manual billing

Handwritten bills get lost, GST calculations take forever, and there's no way to track past transactions.

✅ SOLUTION

Instant digital billing with GST. Create bills in seconds with auto tax calculation, PDF receipts, and complete bill history.

❌ PROBLEM

Scattered tools & WhatsApp groups

Different apps for billing, attendance, inventory. Staff coordination via WhatsApp. No single source of truth.

✅ SOLUTION

One integrated platform. Billing, kitchen display, staff management, inventory, reservations — all in one app with real-time sync.

❌ PROBLEM
